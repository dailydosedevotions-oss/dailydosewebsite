Support

Need Help Now?

Daily Dose can receive prayer requests, but it is not an emergency or crisis service.

If you are in immediate danger

Please contact emergency services in your country now, or go to the nearest safe place and tell someone you trust. In Ireland, call 112 or 999 in an emergency.

If you feel at risk of harming yourself

Please reach out immediately to a trusted person, local emergency services, your GP, a crisis line, or a nearby support service. You do not have to carry that moment alone.

Prayer requests

You are welcome to submit prayer requests through Daily Dose, but prayer messages may not be read instantly. For urgent needs, seek direct help first.
