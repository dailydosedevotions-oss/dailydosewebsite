Daily Dose #19

STAY CONSISTENT

May 9, 2026
This devotion is scheduled and will become available at 7am on its release date.

GALATIANS 6:9 (ESV)

“And let us not grow weary of doing good, for in due season we will reap, if we do not give up.”

Consistency is often tested more than it is talked about. It is not difficult to start something with intention, clarity, and motivation, but it can be difficult to continue when those feelings begin to fade. What once felt clear can begin to feel repetitive, and what once felt strong can begin to feel like effort.

GALATIANS 6:9 (ESV) speaks directly into that tension. Weariness is real, and it does not always come from doing the wrong thing. It can come from doing the right thing over time without seeing immediate results. That can lead to discouragement, questioning, and a temptation to step back or slow down.

Consistency is not sustained by motivation alone. It is sustained by conviction. When something is rooted in truth, it continues even when feelings change. That does not mean it becomes easy, but it does mean there is something deeper holding it in place.

Jesus often pointed to the value of remaining steady rather than reacting to every change in circumstance.

MATTHEW 24:13 (ESV) says, “But the one who endures to the end will be saved.”

That shows that endurance is not about intensity in moments, but about staying the course over time. There is a strength in continuing, even when progress feels slow or unnoticed. What is built through consistency is often not visible immediately, but it is being formed.

There will be days where it feels easier to step back, to ease off, or to question whether it is worth continuing. Those moments are part of the process, not a sign to stop. Continuing in what is right, even when it feels quiet or unseen, is where depth is built.

Consistency is not about perfection.

It is about continuing in the right direction, even when it feels ordinary.

And over time, what feels small and steady begins to produce something that would not exist any other way.
