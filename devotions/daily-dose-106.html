Daily Dose #106

August 8, 2026

A HEART OF THANKSGIVING

This devotion is scheduled and will become available at 7am on August 8, 2026.

PSALM 92:1–2 (ESV)

“It is good to give thanks to the LORD, to sing praises to your name, O Most High; to declare your steadfast love in the morning, and your faithfulness by night.”

Thankfulness is not something we reserve for the days when everything is going well. Scripture teaches us that giving thanks is part of the daily life of every believer. It is a choice we make, not because life is always easy, but because God is always good. Psalm 92 reminds us that “it is good to give thanks to the LORD.” Gratitude is good for our hearts because it keeps our eyes fixed on God instead of allowing our circumstances to control our thinking.

A thankful heart often begins with the small things. We thank God for another day, for the breath in our lungs, for His provision, His mercy, and His presence. As we intentionally begin thanking Him for the ordinary blessings we so easily overlook, something begins to change within us. Our perspective changes, our attitudes begin to soften, and our hearts become more aware of God's goodness throughout the day.

There will always be people or situations that try to rob us of our joy. There may be someone who makes life difficult or circumstances that test our patience. Yet instead of allowing those things to shape our attitude, we can choose to thank God even in the middle of them. We can pray for those who have hurt us, ask God to bless them, and trust Him to work in both their lives and our own. Gratitude has a way of replacing bitterness with peace because it continually reminds us that God is still at work.

As we learn to thank God in the smaller moments of life, we also discover that we are better prepared for the greater challenges that come our way. A thankful heart remembers God's faithfulness yesterday and therefore finds confidence to trust Him today. The God who carried us through smaller trials will also give us the strength to face greater ones. He remains faithful in every season, and every victory we experience ultimately belongs to Him.

Thankfulness is more than saying the words, "Thank You." It is choosing to recognise God's goodness in every season of life and believing that His faithfulness is greater than our circumstances. The more we cultivate a thankful heart, the more we begin to see His hand at work in places we may have once overlooked.

Choose thankfulness today.

Choose to see the goodness of God.

And as you continually give thanks to the Lord, you will discover that gratitude not only changes your perspective, but it also strengthens your faith to trust Him with whatever lies ahead.
