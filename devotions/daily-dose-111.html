Daily Dose #111

August 13, 2026

FOLLOW WHAT IS TRUE

This devotion is scheduled and will become available at 7am on August 13, 2026.

PSALM 85:8 (ESV)

"Let me hear what God the LORD will speak, for he will speak peace to his people."

God does not leave His people without truth. Psalm 85 speaks with a humble posture: "Let me hear what God the LORD will speak." That is the heart of a believer who wants more than noise, more than opinion, and more than self-direction.

What God speaks will never flatter our flesh or lead us into darkness. His Word calls us into truth, repentance, faith, holiness, and rest in Him. Sometimes His Word comforts. Sometimes it corrects. Always it leads with love.

Many other voices compete for the heart. Fear says, "Protect yourself at all costs." Pride says, "You deserve your own way." Shame says, "God is finished with you." But the Word of God speaks differently. It calls us by grace and leads us back to what is true.

To follow what is true, we must stay close. We cannot fill our minds all week with noise and then wonder why truth feels faint. Scripture trains our discernment. Prayer softens our hearts. Obedience keeps us near.

If you feel surrounded by confusion, return to the Lord. Open His Word. Ask Him to quiet what is false and strengthen what is true.

He is faithful, and His truth is safe to follow.
