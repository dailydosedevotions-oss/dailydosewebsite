Daily Dose #20

THE CONDITION OF THE HEART

May 10, 2026
This devotion is scheduled and will become available at 7am on its release date.

PROVERBS 4:23 (ESV)

“Keep your heart with all vigilance, for from it flow the springs of life.”

The condition of the heart shapes more than people often realise. It influences how you think, how you respond, and how you see what is in front of you. It does not always show immediately, but over time what is within begins to affect everything around you.

PROVERBS 4:23 (ESV) makes it clear that the heart is not something to ignore or leave unchecked. It is something to guard. That does not mean closing yourself off, but it does mean being aware of what is taking root inside. Thoughts, attitudes, and responses do not come from nowhere. They develop over time, and what is allowed to stay begins to shape direction.

There are things that can slowly affect the condition of the heart without being obvious at first. Pressure, disappointment, offence, and even comparison can begin to settle in. Left unaddressed, they can change how you see people, how you approach situations, and how you respond in moments that matter.

Jesus consistently brought attention to what was happening beneath the surface, not just what could be seen outwardly.

LUKE 6:45 (ESV) says, “The good person out of the good treasure of his heart produces good, and the evil person out of his evil treasure produces evil…”

That shows that what comes out is connected to what is already within. It is not random. It is the result of what has been allowed to remain.

But the heart is not fixed. It can be shaped, restored, and realigned.

Jesus does not leave a person to manage that alone.

EZEKIEL 36:26 (ESV) says, “And I will give you a new heart, and a new spirit I will put within you…”

That changes the perspective completely. This is not about trying harder to keep everything right internally. It is about allowing God to do a deeper work that brings change from the inside out.

The condition of the heart matters because it affects everything that follows.

And when it is being shaped by God, it begins to produce something steady, healthy, and aligned with where He is leading.
