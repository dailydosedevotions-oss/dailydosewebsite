Privacy

Privacy Policy

How Daily Dose handles information shared through the website.

Information you choose to share

Daily Dose may receive your name, email address, subscription request, prayer request, answered prayer, or message when you submit a form.

Prayer requests

Private prayer requests are sent to Daily Dose and are not displayed publicly. Public prayer requests may be reviewed before appearing on the prayer wall.

Email

If you subscribe, your email may be used to send Daily Dose devotions and related updates. You can unsubscribe at any time through the email service.

App install stats

The site records simple aggregate PWA/app activity, such as install signals and app opens. These stats do not collect names, email addresses, or personal identities.

Contact

For privacy questions, email dailydosedevotions@gmail.com.
