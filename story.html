The Story Behind Daily Dose

Why Daily Dose Exists

The fuller story behind Daily Dose and why these reflections exist.

Daily Dose did not begin as a project, a brand, or an idea to gain attention. It was born in a very broken place.

There was a time in my life where addiction completely consumed me. Years of destruction, hopelessness, confusion and emptiness left me in a place where I genuinely did not know who I was anymore or where my life was heading.

But during treatment, something began to change. Every time I sat in church and listened to the Word of God being preached, it felt personal. It did not feel distant or religious. It felt like God was speaking directly into the condition of my life.

So I started taking notes. Not because I was planning to create anything one day, but because I was trying to hold onto every piece of truth that was helping pull me out of darkness.

Over time, those notes became personal reflections. They carried conviction, healing, surrender, restoration, and learning how to walk with God one day at a time.

Daily Dose exists because the Word of God changed my life. If these reflections can help even one person keep going, return to God, find hope again, or realise they are not too far gone for His grace, then it is worth it.
