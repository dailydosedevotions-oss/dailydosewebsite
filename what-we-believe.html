Foundation

What We Believe

Daily Dose is rooted in Christian faith, Scripture, prayer, grace, repentance, and following Jesus in real life.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, the Saviour of the world, crucified and risen, and the only true source of salvation, forgiveness, healing, and new life.

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the Word of God and the foundation for faith, correction, encouragement, wisdom, and daily discipleship.

Grace and Transformation

We believe no person is too far gone for the grace of God. Real change is possible through surrender, repentance, faith, and the ongoing work of the Holy Spirit.

Prayer

We believe prayer is not performance. It is relationship with God: honest, humble, dependent, and open to His will.

Discipleship

We believe following Jesus is a daily walk. Daily Dose exists to encourage people to stay close to Christ, return to Scripture, and keep walking one day at a time.
