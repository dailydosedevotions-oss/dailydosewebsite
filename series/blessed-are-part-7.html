Daily Dose: Blessed Are

Blessed Are: The Peacemakers - Part 7

Releases Sunday Oct 4, 2026 at 7:00PM

Matthew 5:9 (ESV)

“Blessed are the peacemakers, for they shall be called sons of God.”

Almost everyone says they want peace, but not everyone knows how to pursue peace biblically. Biblical peace goes deeper than avoiding disagreement or keeping things quiet on the surface. Jesus is blessing those who make peace.

A peacemaker brings the character of God into places where bitterness, division, pride, anger, and brokenness try to rule. Biblical peacemaking requires both grace and truth.

Abigail's life in 1 Samuel 25 gives a beautiful example. David and Nabal were moving toward violent conflict, but Abigail stepped into the middle with courage, humility, wisdom, and honour. 1 Samuel 25:32-33 (ESV) records David saying, “Blessed be the LORD... who sent you this day to meet me!” and “Blessed be your discretion.”

Jesus Himself shows us perfect peacemaking. He never compromised truth to please people, but He also never created division through ego, selfishness, or uncontrolled emotion.

Colossians 3:13-15 (ESV) says, “forgiving each other; as the Lord has forgiven you,” and “let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts.”

Barnabas also reflected peacemaking when many believers were afraid of Paul after his conversion. Acts 9:27 (ESV) says, “Barnabas took him and brought him to the apostles.”

At the centre of the gospel is peace with God through Jesus Christ. Romans 5:1 (ESV) says, “we have peace with God through our Lord Jesus Christ.”
